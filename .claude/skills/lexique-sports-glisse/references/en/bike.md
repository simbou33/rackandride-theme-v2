# Bike — Technical lexicon (English)

## Anatomy
- **Frame**: **top tube** (where horizontal wall mounts grab the bike), seat tube, down tube, head tube, seat stays / chain stays. Materials: aluminum, **carbon** (never clamp a carbon frame — cradle/rest only: key rack argument), steel, titanium
- **Fork** (rigid or suspension); **wheels**: rim, spokes, hub; tires (tubeless); diameters: 26", 27.5", **29"** (MTB), **700c** (road/gravel), 20" (BMX, folding)
- **Drivetrain**: derailleur, cassette, chainrings, crankset, chain
- **Cockpit**: **handlebars** (drop bars on road bikes; 780–800 mm wide flat bars on modern MTBs — determines distance from the wall!), stem, levers, hoods
- **Saddle**, seatpost (dropper post on MTBs); **disc brakes** or rim brakes; flat or **clipless pedals**
- **E-bikes**: motor + battery — much heavier; battery often removable (mention it)

## Bike types (weight = the #1 spec for a wall mount)
- **Road bike**: 15–20 lbs (7–9 kg), drop bars, often carbon
- **Gravel bike**: 18–24 lbs (8–11 kg) — hugely trending keyword
- **Hardtail MTB**: 24–31 lbs (11–14 kg)
- **Full-suspension MTB** (trail, enduro, DH): 29–37 lbs (13–17 kg), wide bars
- **E-bike / e-MTB**: 40–62 lbs (18–28 kg) — too heavy for many mounts: always check max load!
- **Commuter / city bike**: 26–40 lbs; fenders and racks in the way
- **Folding bike**; **BMX** (20" wheels, close to skate culture); **fixie / single-speed**: minimalist look, the classic apartment display bike; **vintage steel road bike**: collector wall piece

## Wall mounting vocabulary
- **Horizontal wall mount** (by the top tube, bike parallel to the wall — the decor option)
- **Vertical wall mount / wall hook** (by the front wheel, bike perpendicular — max space saving, garage)
- **Bike hook**, **bike hanger**, **bike storage rack**, multi-bike rack
- Points to address: handlebar width (clearance from wall), fenders/cables, carbon frames, e-bike max load, tires marking the wall

## Rider vocabulary
- Cyclist, roadie, mountain biker, commuter; **bike commuting**; ride, loop, climbing/elevation, singletrack, enduro, downhill (DH), cross-country (XC), bike park, **bikepacking**
- **N+1**: the "correct" number of bikes (current number + 1) — perfect community wink to sell a second mount

## Natural SEO keywords (EN)
bike wall mount, bicycle wall mount, bike wall rack, wall mounted bike rack, vertical bike mount, horizontal bike wall mount, bike hook, hang a bike on the wall, bike storage for apartment, garage bike storage, road bike wall mount, MTB wall mount, e-bike wall mount, bike hanger, space saving bike storage.

## Pitfalls
- Always state max load and contrast road bike (8 kg) vs e-bike (25 kg) — THE buying criterion.
- Wheel sizes: inches for MTB/BMX, 700c for road — don't mix.
